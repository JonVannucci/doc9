_scrollHandlerExtension: Extension = ...

Scrolling within editor will be prevented when a cell is out out viewport. Windowed containers including cells should listen to the scroll request signal and invoke the scrollWithinCell() callback after scrolling the cell back into the view (and after updating the inViewport property).
